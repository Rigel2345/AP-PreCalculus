$x^2+12x+y^2-16y=7$

Complete the square
$(x^2+12x+c_1)+(y^2-16y+c_2)=7 + c_1 + c_2$

$c_1$ will be equal to $(\frac{12}{2})^2$ or in the case $36$
$c_2$ will be equal to $(\frac{-16}{2})^2$ or in this case $64$

$(x^2+12x+36)+(y^2-16y+64)=7 + 36 + 64$
