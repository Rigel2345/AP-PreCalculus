#### Period
$\sin, \cos, \csc, \sec \to 360°, 2\pi$
$\tan, \cot \to 180°, \pi$

---

#### Graph of tan/cot
$y = a\tan(\omega \theta)$
$y = a\cot(\omega \theta)$

Amplitude = $\varnothing$
Period = $\frac{180}{w}$ or $\frac{\pi}{\omega}$

#### Graph of sec/csc
$y = a\sec(\omega \theta)$
$y = a\csc(\omega \theta)$

Amplitude = $\varnothing$
Period = $\frac{360}{w}$ or $\frac{2\pi}{\omega}$
